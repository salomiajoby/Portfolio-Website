About Me

My journey in engineering began with a fascination for how technology can solve real-world problems. As a penultimate year Computer Systems Engineering student at the University of Auckland, I’ve developed a deep passion for software development, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

I enjoy tackling complex problems and transforming innovative ideas into practical solutions. Whether it’s building machine learning models or developing full-stack applications, I thrive on the challenge of creating technology that makes a meaningful impact. I also love participating in hackathons and competitions that challenge my knowledge, push me outside my comfort zone, and provide opportunities to grow, learn, and collaborate with others.

Beyond technology, I am deeply connected to the arts and culture. I have been trained in Indian classical dance and Carnatic singing, completing my Teacher’s Diploma in 2019. These disciplines have instilled in me discipline, perseverance, and creativity qualities that I carry into my work as an engineer. Outside of my studies, I remain actively involved in the arts, which enriches my perspective and fuels my drive to innovate.
